Our Story

From a small room
in Jaipur — to
1000+ happy families

RangBahar started as a side hustle. Our founder Priya, a kurti lover, was tired of overpriced boutiques with boring designs. She started ordering directly from weavers in Jaipur, designing her own pieces — and sharing them on Instagram.

Within months, orders started pouring in from Delhi, Mumbai, Pune and beyond. Today, RangBahar ships across India — keeping it simple: great clothes, real prices, zero middlemen.
